MODULAR KITCHEN – Know more about it !

Why not make the place where you cook your food, an even more interesting space with up market and elegant interiors and accessories . The kitchen is undeniably the heart and soul of our home. It is where we prepare our favorite meal to energize our day. So, it is understandable why we want to make our kitchen look great.

Modular kitchen designs have revolutionized the kitchen decor all over the world. The term `modular 'means that your kitchen is made up of several modules or units. Gone are those days when homes were big and every part of the house had ample space for proper design and furniture installations. With time, houses and apartments are becoming smaller. You can literally go bananas , finding a good , reasonable home for yourself .But what everyone can afford to do is design the available space as per preference. Ensuring that everything is well organized and decently decorated with the available space is the main aim of today’s homeowners. Modular kitchens come to the rescue here.You can clearly make out the difference when you wear designer clothes and clothes made by your neighbourhood tailor . Same is the case with modular and traditional kitchens . Make your cooking experience even more enjoyable , by installing modular kitchen .

Design options for a modular kitchen: Gallery kitchens, island kitchens, and U- and L-shaped kitchens are the most popular options.

  • Gallery kitchens have parallel counters and is the most effective option in the case of space constraints.
  • Island kitchens are designed in such a way that the cooking space is in the middle of the kitchen, storage is on one side, and the other side is open towards the rest of the house.
  • U-shaped kitchens are often a preference of homeowners with a larger kitchen space; they require three adjacent walls.
  • The L-shaped kitchen consists of countertops on two adjoining walls that are perpendicular, forming an L.

Material choices: There are many material options for modular kitchens. People make choices depending upon their preferences and budget. Some common materials include natural wood (of various types like teak, deciduous, maple, and pine), ply, laminates, fiber boards (of which medium-density fiberboard or MDF is widely used), stainless steel, and aluminum. Often, a combination of these can also be used depending upon the design preferences.

With time, houses and apartments are becoming smaller. So the need of the hour is to utilize the given small space in an efficient manner and making the kitchen look more spacious .Here are certain tips which will make your kitchen look more spacious and at the same time will save your money too.

  • Marine ply in kitchen Plywood inside any area of your home that gets wet frequently should be marine . Marine ply are costlier than the other ones but will last longer as compared to the other types of ply .
  • Stain steel hinges below sink Sink gets in touch with water all the time .So make sure the hinges below sink are of stainless steel , otherwise they will rust with time . They are a little costlier than the regular hinges , but in long run , they will pay off.
  • Parallel kitchen platformsParallel kitchen platforms are the best . In L shape , the corner never gets utilized properly .Parallel kitchens offer best functionality for using your kitchen efficiently .Utlisation of space and ease of usage is high .
  • Kitchen triangle When you prepare your meals , you mainly use 3 things:
    • USE SINK
    • USECOOKING RANGE
    • USE FRIDGE

Hence , every kitchen has 3 main corners and when you use your kitchen , you are moving between these corners .For efficient kitchen , make sure triangle is neither too big too small nor too small and there are no obstructing objects in the middle of this triangle .Keep an over-the-sink cutting board handy to temporarily expand your counter space.

  • Create extra counter space by buying an over-the-sink shelf.
  • Install a hanging rack, and keep kitchen essentials out of the way but within easy reach.
  • When organizing your kitchen, it’s easy to forget the dark area below the sink. Stop tossing in the miscellaneous cleaning supplies, and give them a space that’s all their own.
  • Simply going with a single rather than double bowl for the sink can save you a foot or more of counter space.
  • Install cabinets on both sides of the kitchen .

Why should you go for a modular kitchen ?

  • If there's any damage, then only that one unit has to be repaired or replaced.
  • Modular kitchens optimize space .
  • Modular kitchens come in a whole range of colours in bold colours of reds, gold, blues, oranges and greens apart from wood shades like cedar and birch. Be sure the material you opt for cabinet is water resistant.While selecting counters for modular kitchen granite is the best option.
  • Cleaning modular kitchens is a very easy and time saving task.
  • Replacement/repairs are easy as the modules or units made in the modular kitchen systems all over the world are in the same standardized size.
  • They can be built externally while other work is completed.
